# Hello World

Welcome to my new portfolio blog! This is a sample post to demonstrate the power of **MDX** with **Next.js**.

## Why MDX?

MDX allows you to write Markdown with embedded React components. This means you can:

- Use custom components like `<Callout />` or `<Button />` directly in your markdown.
- maintain a consistent design system.
- Write interactive content easily.
